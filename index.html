Radial gradient generator

On the left you have a panel with several buttons.

- this button will show you the generated code of your gradient

bg - click on this button to set the background color

- click on this button to add new gradient do canvas

After adding at least one gradient, you will be able to clear canvas by clicking on the button

On the right side you have a button It shows a library with saved gradients. You can save your current work in it, but also load previously saved gradients from it.

You can resize the canvas you are working on. To do this grab its bottom right corner. Saving the gradient to the library will save it along with the size of the canvas.

After you create your gradient, you can save it to png by clicking button.
